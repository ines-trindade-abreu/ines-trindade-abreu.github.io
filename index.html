In Brief

A Wanderer Between Disciplines

I am a graduate researcher at Técnico Lisboa, working at the intersection of nuclear astrophysics and the history and philosophy of science. My work concerns how knowledge is made, transmitted, and transformed — from the interior of stars to the networks of scholars who first decoded them.

Alongside my research, I write code, build things, and spend a perhaps unreasonable amount of time thinking about medieval manuscripts, spectral lines, and the long biography of the atom.
